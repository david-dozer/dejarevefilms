Deja Reve Films LLC.

Deja Reve Films is a film production company based in Chicago, Illinois, specializing in narrative films. Formed in 2024, Deja Reve Films aspires to elevate the industry standard in storytelling and the art of precision filmmaking. From pre-production to post-production, we aim to excel in creating the highest quality films known to man.

Christian Mendoza

Christian Mendoza is a producer, screenwriter, and director based out of Palatine, Illinois. He is the founder and owner of Deja Reve Films. Specializing in writing and directing, he is mostly self-taught as a filmmaker and has learned from hands-experience working as a production assistant in feature film productions. In 2016, Christian enlisted in the Illinois Army National Guard and served as a unit supply specialist with A Battery 2-122 33rd IBCT for six years. While he's not making films, he trains jiu jitsu at Real Movement and Martial Arts (RMMA) as a white belt under Joey Diehl.
